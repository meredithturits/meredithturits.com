About Meredith

Meredith Turits was born in New York, and spent most of her youth riding the 6 train in and out of the East Village. She currently lives and writes in Park Slope, Brooklyn—and doesn’t yet own a stroller.

She is a magna cum laude graduate of Tufts University, where she studied Philosophy of Language and Linguistics. She earned a minor in Communications and Media Studies through Tufts and the School of the Museum of Fine Arts in Boston. Meredith is the Online Associate Editor at Glamour magazine, where she oversees the site’s health/fitness and weddings channels, and Inspired, the inspiring women/politics channel. She spearheaded its launch in early 2012, and is lead writer and top editor of its anchor blog, The Conversation, which has introduced content including authors, current events, and influential women to the site's daily editorial lineup. She is also the author of a weekly relationships column on Smitten, Glamour.com’s sex and love vertical.

Meredith has also created a platform around her name outside of her work. Her personal blog, Such Small Hands, has several thousand followers, and she is an editor for Tumblr’s Prose, Lit, and Poetry tags. She tweets at @meredithturits.

Her short fiction has appeared in Anobium Literary Magazine, Millennials Magazine, Podium Literary Journal, and The Public Journal. Her non-fiction, reporting, and personal essays have appeared in publications including Glamour.com, Gothamist, Effed in Park Slope, Performer magazine, and Skyscraper magazine. She is at work on a second novel, and continues to write personal essays, profiles, and non-fiction articles, two of which are forthcoming in Glamour magazine.

Meredith is (honestly) delighted to hear from you with opportunities, questions, comments, or to simply say hi. She (also honestly) asks that you do not contact her gmail address for inquires relating to Glamour, and direct all PR pitches through her Condé Nast email.
