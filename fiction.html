Meredith Turits

Fiction

Meredith's short fiction has been published both in print and online. Her first novel, EVERYTHING I WANT YOU TO BE, is a complete manuscript of literary fiction, and she is currently working on a second novel.

She is actively seeking literary representation.

Novel synopsis:

EVERYTHING I WANT YOU TO BE
Length: 100,000 words

In Everything I Want You to Be, main character CHRISTIAN’s aim is concrete: hook the vulnerable girl and spend two weeks crafting the flawless world she never thought she deserved. Then, destroy it all. 28-year-old Christian’s serial seduction is his only outlet to take control over his life—a power he feels was stripped away from him by his twin sister, CAMILLE. Growing up in Brussels with inattentive parents, the twins developed an indelible bond—but Christian took it too far, and everything he knew collapsed. Having rebuilt a life in Brooklyn with his own set of parameters, Christian has rewritten his past; his sister doesn’t exist, and nor does his obsessive history with her. Now, there are only the city streets and the new girls.

Christian’s past with his sister unfolds as the novel follows him through his present relationship with one seductee. When he meets PAIGE, the redheaded art gallery assistant, everything begins on course, but a significant parallel from her life that strikes a chord in Christian won’t let him sever the tie. His formula isn’t working, yet he refuses to accept that his rules are incompatible with reality. As his lies unravel and past and present threaten to converge, Christian must decide where his secrets actually belong: out in a world where honesty could allow him a future with Paige, or hidden away in his fabricated version of reality.
